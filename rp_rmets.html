Science Engagement Fellowship

In June 2022 I started a 1 year role as a Royal Meteorological Society (RMetS) Science Engagement Fellow. This is a fantastic opportunity to support RMetS in the delivery of their Climate Action Plan but also to further develop my network of industry stakeholder and leading academics. Three new special interest groups are being developed. These focus on:

  • The Energy Sector.
  • The insurance Sector.
  • Early Career Researchers.

I am leading the development of the insurance special interest group. Please see the links below for more information on future activities that RMetS are offereing that may be of relevance if you work within the sector!

What is the Royal Meteorological Society?

The Royal Meteorological Society works to strengthen the science and raise awareness of the importance of weather and climate, support meteorological professionals and inspire enthusiasts. Anyone who is interested or involved in meteorology or associated sciences can join. The worldwide membership is made up of professionals and academics, students and teachers, enthusiasts and observers, and there is a category to suit everyone.

Check out the website for more information

Meteorological Masterclasses

RMetS reguarly host free CPD training sessions on topical weather and climate phenomena. For example in winter 2021-22 there are masterclasses on North Atlantic and European Cyclones and The Role of the Jet Stream in Weather, Predictability, and Climate. Check out the website for upcoming events.
